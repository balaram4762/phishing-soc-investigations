# SOC Investigation Ticket — PHI-001

Case ID: PHI-001
Date: 2026-06-29
Severity: HIGH
Attack Type: Credential Phishing

## What this email/site was about
This site was made to look like Sahibinden, a Turkish online shopping platform. The domain used was sahibinden.elektronik-magaza-sepetim.com, which is a fake lookalike domain trying to trick users into thinking it's the real Sahibinden site.

## What I did and found
I checked the URL on VirusTotal and 4 out of 92 antivirus vendors flagged it as phishing, including Emsisoft, Fortinet, Netcraft and SOCRadar. The domain was registered on the same day it was reported, which is a common pattern in phishing — attackers register a domain, use it quickly, then move on before it gets taken down. I also checked the server IP (104.21.20.125) and found it's hosted behind Cloudflare, which attackers often use to hide where the site is really hosted.

## IOCs found
- Domain: sahibinden.elektronik-magaza-sepetim.com
- URL: http://sahibinden.elektronik-magaza-sepetim.com/adres
- IP: 104.21.20.125

## What I'd recommend
Block this domain at the firewall, report the URL to PhishTank, and warn any users who may have clicked the link.
