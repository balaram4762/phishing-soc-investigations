# SOC Investigation Ticket — PHI-003

Case ID: PHI-003
Date: 2026-06-29
Severity: CRITICAL
Attack Type: Credential Phishing

## What this email/site was about
This site (unitedmedicalsolutions.org) was set up to look like a medical services provider. It doesn't impersonate one specific well-known brand, but uses a generic "trusted medical company" look to get people to enter personal or payment information.

## What I did and found
This one had the strongest detection of all 5 cases — 16 out of 92 vendors on VirusTotal flagged it as malicious, which means it's a confirmed, actively tracked phishing site, not a borderline case. The server IP (216.55.149.9) is registered to a company called "Internet Names For Business Inc.," and that IP itself was also flagged by 1 vendor. Given the high detection count, this is one of the more dangerous samples I looked at.

## IOCs found
- Domain: unitedmedicalsolutions.org
- URL: https://unitedmedicalsolutions.org
- IP: 216.55.149.9

## What I'd recommend
This should be blocked immediately at both the domain and IP level since it has the highest detection score in this batch. Also worth reporting to VirusTotal's community for further visibility.
