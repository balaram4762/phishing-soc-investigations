## What this email/site was about
This site impersonated Smart Fit, a gym/fitness chain. The URL had Google Ads tracking parameters in it (gad_source, gad_campaignid), which suggests this phishing page was being pushed through paid Google ads rather than email — a less common but effective method since people trust ads that show up in search results.

## What I did and found
VirusTotal flagged it with 5 out of 92 vendors. The hosting IP was 172.67.169.143. The use of fake Google Ads tracking parameters stood out to me here — it means whoever made this either bought ads pointing to this domain or copied a real ad URL structure to make the link look more convincing when shared.

## IOCs found
- Domain: smartfitbr.com
- URL: https://www.smartfitbr.com/?gad_source=1&gad_campaignid=2392987030
- IP: 172.67.169.143

## What I'd recommend
Block the domain, and if this was actually running as a paid ad, report the campaign to Google Ads abuse team so they can pull it down at the source.
