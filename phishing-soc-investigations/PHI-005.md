## Email Details
- **Subject:** Smart Fit fake promotional/ad page
- **Sender:** `smartfitbr.com`

## Summary of Findings
Fake website impersonating Smart Fit, a real fitness/gym brand, 
using a Google Ads campaign link structure to appear legitimate. 
Flagged by 5 out of 92 security vendors on VirusTotal as malicious. 
Server IP 172.67.169.143 hosting the malicious content. This 
attack type uses paid advertising click-throughs (gad_source 
parameter) to lure victims into a fake login or payment page.

## Tools Used
- VirusTotal
- PhishTank

### IP Addresses
- `172.67.169.143`

### Domains
- `smartfitbr.com`

### URLs
- `https://www.smartfitbr.com/?gad_source=1&gad_campaignid=2392987030`

### Sender Email Addresses
- None identified

### File Hashes (MD5 / SHA256)
- None identified

## Recommendation
Block domain smartfitbr.com at firewall.
Report fraudulent Google Ads campaign to Google Ads abuse team.
Alert users who may have clicked the ad and entered payment details.
