# SOC Investigation Ticket — PHI-004

Case ID: PHI-004
Date: 2026-06-29
Severity: HIGH
Attack Type: Credential Phishing

## What this email/site was about
This site (icloud-unlock-bypass.com) pretends to offer a service to "bypass" or unlock locked iCloud accounts. This is a known scam pattern — people who lost access to an Apple device search for unlock services and land on fake sites like this, which either charge them for a fake service or try to steal their Apple ID credentials.

## What I did and found
VirusTotal flagged this URL with 3 out of 90 vendors as malicious. I checked the hosting IP (69.16.230.228) and it was also flagged by 1 out of 90 vendors. Lower detection count than some of the other cases, but the domain name itself ("icloud-unlock-bypass") is a strong giveaway — Apple does not offer or endorse any third-party unlock bypass service, so any site offering this is automatically suspicious regardless of detection score.

## IOCs found
- Domain: icloud-unlock-bypass.com
- URL: http://www.icloud-unlock-bypass.com
- IP: 69.16.230.228

## What I'd recommend
Block the domain and IP, and if any users searched for "iCloud unlock" recently, it's worth checking if they visited this or similar sites.
